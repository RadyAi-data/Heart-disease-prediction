# Heart Disease Prediction Model Evaluation

## Dataset Information
- Source: UCI Heart Disease Dataset
- Samples: 303
- Features: 13 clinical parameters
- Target: Presence of heart disease (0 = no, 1 = yes)

## Model Performance

### Random Forest Classifier
- Accuracy: 0.85
- Precision: 0.84
- Recall: 0.88
- F1-score: 0.86
- ROC AUC: 0.93

### Logistic Regression
- Accuracy: 0.82
- Precision: 0.81
- Recall: 0.85
- F1-score: 0.83
- ROC AUC: 0.89

### Support Vector Machine
- Accuracy: 0.83
- Precision: 0.82
- Recall: 0.86
- F1-score: 0.84
- ROC AUC: 0.90

## Feature Importance
1. thal (thalassemia)
2. ca (number of major vessels)
3. cp (chest pain type)
4. oldpeak (ST depression)
5. thalach (max heart rate)

## Conclusion
The Random Forest model performed best with 85% accuracy and will be used in the final application.
